Frequently Asked Course Questions

Have questions like:

Where do I find my grades?
Or How often do I need to check in Online?
Find answers and guidance in the sections below.

How Do I Contact the Facilitator?

All questions and comments regarding the course should be posted in the Questions or Comments forum. Facilitators monitor the Questions or Comments forum on a regular basis. If you wish to contact your facilitator privately, feel free to send them an email.

How Do I Communicate with my Classmates?

Communicate with other students in the class by posting course-related messages in the Questions or Comments forum or social messages in the General Forums. Please pay attention to the following:

  • Review all News Announcements on a regular basis
  • Check the Questions or Comments forum regularly
  • Feel free to communicate with other classmates on any of the discussion forums.
  • Read the messages posted by other students since the forums are likely to contain questions and comments that may benefit you.

How Often Do I Need to Check in Online?

Regularly check the Questions or Comments forum (and your Workgroup forum if applicable) for communications from your facilitator and/or classmates. We recommend you check the forums daily or every other day for 10-20 minutes in order to remain up to date.

I Don't Have Course Resources, How Do I Find Them?

Many OntarioLearn courses require a textbook. For details on how to obtain your textbooks, refer to the information package provided by your registering college. This information package may have been sent to you through regular mail, email, or posted on your registering college's web site.

Your course textbooks should be available for purchase at your registering college bookstore. If you are encountering difficulties obtaining your textbooks, please contact the OntarioLearn Coordinator at your registering college.

If the textbook is unavailable from your college bookstore, Durham College's bookstore may have additional copies that can be ordered online. Click on the appropriate semester and then Durham College’s online course code.

Take note of the Return Policy when you purchase your textbooks.

How Do I Submit Assignments/Tests?

Assignments and tests are posted within each weekly module of your course, including details on how to submit the completed work. You will also find them by clicking on Activities at the top of the screen.

Due dates and a course overview are posted in the Course Overview and Schedule module.

How Do I Find My Marks?

For feedback and grades, click on the Grades button found along the top navigation bar.

Final Marks

Facilitators do not send out final marks or exam marks to students. You receive your final grades from your registering college. This may take up to 6 weeks after course completion. If you need a final mark sooner, you must contact your registering college. Please do not ask your facilitator for the final mark.

What is the Passing Grade for this Course?

Contact your registering college for information on the passing grade for this course. This mark may vary according to different programs. Facilitators cannot tell you if you passed or failed.
